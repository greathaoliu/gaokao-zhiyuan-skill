# Defaults And Intake Factors

Use this reference when the user is unsure, does not want to answer every question, or gives vague preferences. The goal is to keep the consultation moving while making assumptions visible.

## Do Not Default These Facts

Do not produce a final plan without these:

- Province.
- 2026 or explicit target year.
- Candidate category/batch.
- Selected subjects or科类.
- Score and rank/位次 for rank-based recommendations.
- Official province/batch rules and volunteer model.

If these are missing, ask the next single question instead of guessing.

## Conservative Defaults For Vague Preferences

When the user says "都行", "不知道", "你看着办", or skips a preference, use these defaults and disclose them.

| Field | Default | Why |
|---|---|---|

| 中外合作/高收费 | 默认不接受高收费项目 | Avoid major fee risk |
| 家庭预算 | 默认优先公办普通收费 | Safer than assuming high budget |

## Disclosure Pattern

Before a draft recommendation, include:

```text
我先替你默认了这些偏好，后面都可以改：
- 民办/中外合作：默认不接受高收费项目
- 风险偏好：默认中等偏稳
- 职业路径：默认本科就业和考研两手准备
```

Use `默认` for assumptions, `待确认` for facts that cannot be safely assumed, and `已核验` for official data.

## Broader Intake Factors To Consider

Cover these when they materially affect recommendations:

- Basic: province, year, selected subjects, score, rank, category/batch.
- Family: budget, private college, Sino-foreign/co-op, postgraduate support, family employment resources.
- City: preferred cities, acceptable regions, rejected regions, distance from home.
- Major: priority majors, acceptable majors, caution majors, rejected majors, broad-category admission, adjustment risk.
- Personal fit: strong subjects, weak subjects, coding tolerance, memorization tolerance, long-term study tolerance, personality and pressure tolerance.
- Career: stability, high salary, city opportunity, public-sector exam, state-owned enterprise, hospital, power grid, finance, internet, semiconductor, teacher track.
- Further study: postgraduate, overseas study, doctoral path.
- Risk: school-first, major-first, city-first, employment-first, conservative anti-slip,冲稳保 ratio, unacceptable outcome.
- Special programs: military/police,公费师范,医学定向,专项计划,提前批.
- Restrictions: physical exam, color vision, eyesight, height, language, single-subject scores.
- Final remarks: parent opinion and student opinion.

## Interaction Rule

Prefer one question per turn. Use defaults to avoid exhausting the user, but never hide that defaults were used.
