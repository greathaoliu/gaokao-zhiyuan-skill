# Risk Checklist

## Common Risks

Check these before calling a plan "ready":

- 滑档：all choices are too aggressive or lack conservative options.
- 退档：投档后不满足专业录取、体检、单科、语种、调剂等条件.
- 专业风险：被调剂到 unacceptable majors, broad major categories with later diversion, or major transfer difficulty.
- 代码风险：wrong institution code, major code, campus code, or program type.
- 校区风险：same school has different campuses, cities, tuition, or training models.
- 费用风险：private, independent college, Sino-foreign/co-op, software, art, or special programs may cost much more.
- 选科风险：new-Gaokao subject requirements differ by major/group/year.
- 身体条件风险：medical, color vision, height, hearing, smell, hepatitis-related restrictions where lawful and stated.
- 语种风险：English-only teaching, non-English candidates, oral test requirements.
- 单科风险：math/English/Chinese/related-subject minimum scores.
- 就业认知风险：major names sound similar but curriculum and career path differ.
- 数据误读风险：school-level minimum line used for a major-level decision.
- 培养方案风险：招生宣传页与教务处/学院培养方案不一致, or the major is split into directions after enrollment.
- 就业数据风险：using media rankings, marketing claims, or third-party就业 summaries instead of official就业质量报告 and college career-center information.

## Final Pre-Submission Check

Use this compact checklist:

```text
最终核验：
- [ ] 省考试院规则、时间、批次、志愿数量已核对
- [ ] 所有代码来自官方计划书/系统
- [ ] 每个志愿单位的选科要求满足
- [ ] 招生章程限制已读完并标注风险
- [ ] 学费、校区、培养模式确认
- [ ] 专业培养方案、转专业/大类分流、官方就业质量信息已核验
- [ ] 冲稳保梯度与位次证据一致
- [ ] 保底志愿足够保守
- [ ] 家庭无法接受的专业/城市/费用已排除
- [ ] 最终顺序符合“最想去且可承受风险”的原则
```

## Risk Wording

Use precise wording:

- Say "这个选项需要核验招生章程中的体检限制" instead of "不能报".
- Say "按现有位次证据偏冲" instead of "一定能上/一定不能上".
- Say "第三方数据仅作筛选，最终以省考试院和院校官方为准" when non-official sources are involved.
